Our Planet has provided us with abundance of gifts like the nature, animals and resorces.
Now the planet is in danger, we are damaging the eco system.
Earth is mainly affected by all the three ways, by pollution, plastic and Global Warming.
The human activities, rich company and corruption of the goverment are provoking the Fury Nature,
and One day the planet we lived over centuries come to the state of peril.
Is time to protect this world to change, to make a better place for our children.
We can't start from here:

1. Avoid devastating the nature by cutting down trees unnecessarily.
It's though tough due to growing population but we can atleast plant trees by
ourselves and take care of it in empty parks or gardens.

2. Ban using Plastics. They have a toxic substance causing health detriment and animals and humans get affected.
Emits harmful chemicals damaging the soli and water: So, reduce, reuse and recycle.

3. Try to use a eco-friendly vehicle like cycles instead of cars, where you don't need.

4. Save Electricity.
Endeavor to use renewable energy.
Ensure to use Air conditioners and refrigerators judiciously.

5. Conserve Water. The little things can make a big difference.

We have to try our best to save our planet, start a fresh life by saving both our Home and ourselves.
